Our ongoing commitment to education and development continues to drive us toward creating lasting positive change in Nyali Constituency.

Mission Statement:
To lead and implement sustainable, high-impact community development initiatives in Nyali Constituency that empower individuals and families, fostering economic growth, social inclusion, and a united community with the capacity to thrive independently.

Our vision:
To create a self-sustaining community in Nyali Constituency, where every family and individual enjoys a brighter future, enriched by improved quality of life, economic stability, and social wellbeing.

History of our Foundation

  • The inception of ANHF was also inspired by the growing interest and encouragement from both local and international humanitarian organizations operating in Nyali Constituency. These institutions had long advocated for the emergence of youth-led organizations focused on development, education, policy advocacy, and leadership. ANHF was founded to fill this void and to channel the energy, creativity, and passion of young people towards meaningful community-driven initiatives
  • From the outset, ANHF has been committed to addressing the critical needs of the community, with a particular focus on youth, children, women, widows, and people with disabilities. The foundation was established in response to the persistent challenges faced by these groups, including inadequate access to resources, lack of opportunities, and the absence of effective interventions from both government and non-governmental entitie
  • Unlike many organizations that have struggled with limited resources and ineffective policies, ANHF is determined to make a real difference. We are dedicated to fostering sustainable development through the power of volunteerism, intercultural exchange, and community engagement. Our programs are designed to promote youth employment, social inclusion, and the unity of purpose among diverse communities
